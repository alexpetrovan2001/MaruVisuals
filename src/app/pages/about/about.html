About Marius Muresan

Marius is a photographer, videographer, and creative director specializing in premium visual content for the hospitality and lifestyle industry. He collaborates with hotels, resorts, and brands to craft cinematic imagery and films that highlight the essence of each location and experience.

Combining storytelling with a refined aesthetic, he focuses on creating visuals that feel natural and immersive — guided by light, emotion, and atmosphere. Beyond capturing visuals, Marius also develops the creative direction, from concept and narrative design to the final visual outcome, ensuring that every project reflects the brand’s identity in a cohesive and authentic way.

Through his thoughtful approach and attention to detail, he helps brands communicate their essence with elegance and depth.

About Ioana Manu

Ioana is a photographer and scriptwriter specialized in creating visual narratives for hotels, resorts, and lifestyle brands. With a background in pharmacy, she brings a unique combination of precision, empathy, and creative structure to her work, qualities that translate into visuals rich in detail and emotion.

She collaborates with both hospitality destinations and product-based brands, focusing on transforming concepts into cinematic, authentic stories.

Ioana joined MARU in 2024, adding a new layer of creativity and narrative depth to the team. Her approach blends artistic vision with thoughtful storytelling, bringing clarity and purpose to every project she contributes to.
